Professional Experience

With a strong IT background and hands-on experience from my internship, I enjoy learning new technologies and skills.

Currently, I'm working on an NFC scanner for hotels and businesses, with 1,500 cards ready for configuration.

At the same time, I'm helping to code elevator cameras to counteract the government's practice of making deals with businesses to maximize their profits.

In my country, if a business sells something at a low cost but in high volume, the government doesn't benefit as much.

Instead, they make deals where businesses sell products at nearly four times the actual cost, giving the government half of the income in exchange for market access.

This leads to building devices being sold at near-military prices.
